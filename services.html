Sompower Services

Power Generation

Power generation is the first component of the electricity services of Sompower. Our electricity is generated from diesel fueled generators and solar farms. B-station & sheedaha are the main stations which are currently on the line and supplying to most of Hargiesa and surroundings.Sompower has implemented 2.5MW solar farm which is the biggest renewable energy generation site in Somaliland at large.

Power Transmission

Sompower is currently supplying through 11KV transmission line throughout Hargeis and surroundings. Electricity is regularly transmitted to substations near populated areas.The main components of our transmission system are:-

  • Transmission concrete poles
  • Transmission lines overhead/underground
  • Substation transformers

Power Distribution

Distribution is the final component of the Power of Sompower to serve its consumers. Sompower is the major distribution company which supplies over 90% of Hargeisa and surrounding regions. Sompower distribution system contains of:-

  • Transmission concrete poles
  • Transmission lines overhead/underground
  • Substation transformers
